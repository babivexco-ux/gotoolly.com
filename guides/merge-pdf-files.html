Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a limit to how many PDFs I can merge?

Our tool can merge any number of PDFs, limited only by your device's memory and performance. For optimal results, we recommend merging up to 20 PDFs at once, or larger batches in multiple sessions if needed.

Will the merged PDF maintain original quality?

Yes! Our merging process preserves the original quality of all PDFs. Images, text, formatting, and fonts remain exactly as in your original documents. No compression or quality loss occurs during merging.

Can I merge password-protected PDFs?

For security reasons, password-protected PDFs cannot be merged. Please remove passwords from your PDFs first using appropriate software, then merge the unprotected versions.

What's the maximum file size for merging?

PDFs up to 25MB each can be processed efficiently. Larger files may require more processing time depending on your device's capabilities. There's no total size limit for the merged document.

Can I reorder pages after merging?

Our tool allows you to reorder entire PDFs before merging, but not individual pages within PDFs. For page-level reordering, use our PDF Splitter & Rotator first, then merge in your desired order.
