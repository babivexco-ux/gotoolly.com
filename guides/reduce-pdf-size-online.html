Frequently Asked Questions

Will compression damage my PDF quality?

Our tool uses intelligent compression that prioritizes text clarity. Text remains sharp, while images are optimized based on your chosen compression level. Always preview the result before finalizing.

How much can I reduce my PDF size?

Text-heavy PDFs can be reduced by 80-90%. Image-heavy PDFs typically see 50-80% reduction. Scanned documents achieve 60-85% reduction while maintaining readability.

Can I compress password-protected PDFs?

For security reasons, password-protected PDFs cannot be compressed. Please remove passwords first using appropriate software, then compress the unprotected version.

What's the maximum file size for compression?

PDFs up to 50MB can be compressed efficiently. Larger files may require more processing time depending on your device's capabilities.

Should I compress before or after merging PDFs?

Compress individual files first, then merge them. This approach gives you more control over quality and typically results in better overall compression.
